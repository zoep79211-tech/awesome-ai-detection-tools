Winston AI differentiates itself primarily through multilingual support and the pairing of AI detection with a human readability score. While most detectors return a single AI probability percentage, Winston adds a Flesch readability grade to every report — a useful signal for editors who care about content quality beyond just AI origin.

The tool supports uploads in PDF, DOCX, and plain text, which reduces friction for editorial workflows where documents arrive in different formats. The sentence-level highlighting uses a colour gradient from green (likely human) through yellow to red (likely AI), making it easy to identify the specific passages that need review.

The API is a differentiator for teams that want to embed detection into their own editorial or publishing tools. The endpoints are straightforward and well-documented, and the response includes both the overall score and per-sentence breakdowns.

Language support covers English, French, Spanish, German, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Russian, and several others — making it one of the more capable options for non-English content. Accuracy on non-English text is generally lower than on English, which is true across the category, but Winston's multilingual model is more mature than most competitors.

The free tier allows a limited number of words per month. The Pro plan removes limits and adds the API. An Enterprise tier includes white-labelling and custom integrations.
